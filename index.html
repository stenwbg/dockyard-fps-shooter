Briefing

Dockyard OPS

Mission. Clear each sector wave by wave. Survive, manage ammo, and use grenades on groups or red explosive drums. The rifle reaches farther; the shotgun hits harder up close.

Progress. You begin in an abandoned industrial yard, push into liminal corridors, then a graveyard sector. After that, an in-service metro hall and an open-air construction site await—same haunted-industrial tone, new ground to hold.

Controls. WASD move · Space jump · LMB fire · R reload · 1–4 weapons · 3/G grenade · P pause.
